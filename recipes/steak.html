Steak Recipe

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Heat up cast iron skillet
  3. Season steak with salt and pepper generously. Make sure to cover all sides
  4. Place steak in hot pan
  5. Place butter and thyme and baste steak after flipping
  6. Let steak rest after cooking for 100 minutes
